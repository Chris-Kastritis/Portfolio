About Me


I've always had a passion for problem-solving. As a kid, I spent countless hours working through logic puzzle books my parents gave me just for fun. That same curiosity and love for challenges naturally led me to programming, which I discovered in my first high school coding class. From that moment on, I took every programming course available because I knew there was nothing else I wanted to do more in my life.


This enthusiasm carried through to college, where I worked hard early on and positioned myself to graduate with my bachelor's degree in just three years. Instead of rushing through, I chose to join my university’s accelerated bachelor’s/master’s program. This allowed me to slow down a bit and earn my master’s degree with just one additional year of study. Thanks to this decision and my dedication, I graduated with both a Bachelor’s and Master’s degree in Computer Science in just four and a half years.
